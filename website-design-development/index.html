Web design & development

I love to design and build lightning-fast, responsive, future-proof websites which both look great but are also easy to use and focused; focused on helping a business achieve their goals and focused on helping meet the needs of a website's users.

The process

The starting point is always getting to properly understand the objectives that we're working towards or the problems that we're trying to solve. It's only then that mapping out a solution can be as well considered as it needs to be. Once we have a solution mapped out, we can decide on the best options for design and for the technology we use to suit your budget.

The overall process can be summarised as:

  • Understand the project's goals
  • Map out a solution with sitemap / wireframes / mockups as appropriate
  • Align options with budget
  • Design / refine / build the solution
  • Test and review
  • Go live!
  • Measure and adjust to make sure that goals are being met
